ABOUT

Welcome to Birds on Booze Bottles!

We collect images of booze labels of all kinds - beer, wine, cider, and hard liquor - as long as they have a (you guessed it) bird on the label. Our goal is to display images of beverages from around the world and we accept submissions from anyone. Know of a bird-adorned beverage not yet in the Birds on Booze Bottles collection? Head over to the Contact page and send it our way!

SITE NAVIGATION

Home Page

The home page of Birds on Booze Bottles is a place to explore all the labels in our collection. See an image you like? Click on it and it'll lead you to a page with more information, including:

  • An image of the label
  • The name of the beverage
  • The product website (in case you want to learn more)
  • The name of the company that makes it
  • What country it's made in
  • The type of beverage
  • What type of bird is on the label (booze can be educational! who knew?)

Explore Pages

Enjoying exploring the home page but wishing you could narrow things down? We've got you.

The Explore menu gives people a place to view our collection organized by:

  • Bird type
  • Booze type
  • Country of origin

Examples

  • Want to see only labels with owls on them? Navigate through the dropdown menu to Explore > By Bird > Owls
  • Curious what the labels look like for all varieties of IPA? Navigate through the dropdown menu to Explore > By Beverage > Beer > IPA
  • Itching to know what labels from only Australia look like? Navigate through the dropdown menu to Explore > By Country > Australia

Contact Page

Have a label you want to submit to be added to the Birds on Booze Bottles collection? Spotted a correction that needs to be made? Got a question or just want to say Hello?

We've got just the place!

Using the Contact page, you can get in touch and can even submit images of new labels you'd like submitted to the collection. Just give us your name, an email address we can get back to you at, and attach an optional image. We'll get in touch as soon as possible.

Cheers!
